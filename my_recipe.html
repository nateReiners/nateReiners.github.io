Nate's Signature Chicken Burritos

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs. frozen chicken thigh meat
  • 4 cans refried beans
  • 20 large flour tortillas
  • 16 oz. shredded jack cheese
  • salsa (optional)
  • guacamole (optional)

Chicken Burritos

Description

These are the cheapest chicken burritos that you can make. Absolutely better than eating just plain tortillas or cold refried beans.

Instructions

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees (F)
  3. Bake chicken in baking pan at 400 degrees for 40 minutes
  4. Open can of refried beans
  5. Construct burritos with perfect ratio of bean slop and delicious chicken.
  6. Freeze burritos and microwave for 2:30 at 80% power
  7. Add hot sauce or salsa to taste.
  8. Enjoy!
